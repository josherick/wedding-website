Our Story
Abridged
Megs and Josh met in college at the University of Michigan. After meeting in a learning community (MCSP) during Megs' freshman year, they started dating two years later when they lived in a house together off campus.
After Josh graduated, he moved to Seattle, and Megs moved to Washington, D.C. a year after. They had a long distance relationship for two years, until Josh moved to New York (then it became a short distance relationship).
After living together in Michigan during COVID, and another stint in D.C., Megs moved to Brooklyn to live with Josh. Josh proposed to Megs in Domino Park in July 2024!
