Accommodation Options

We encourage you to book accommodations once you've decided to join us at our wedding, as many options book up 6+ months in advance.

Affordable and Convenient Hotel Blocks

Hotels book up early in the Leelenau Peninsula area, so we've reserved several room blocks for our guests. We will have shuttle busses to and from these hotels to our venue.

  • Leelanau Sands Casino - Affordable and very close to the venue
  • Bayshore Traverse City - Affordable, beachfront, and walkable to downtown Traverse City
  • Boardwalk Suites - A bit pricier, in downtown Traverse City

Boutique Hotels and Local Airbnbs

If you're looking for some lovely boutique hotels, we'd recommend the following.

  • Whaleback Inn
  • Riverside Inn
  • Falling Waters Lodge
  • Leland Lodge
  • Sunrise Landing
  • The Vinyard Inn
  • M22 Inn
  • The Mill (30 min drive but very nice)

If you have a larger group, it's also worth checking Airbnb and VRBO to see what is available in the area.

If you look at other options, we love Leelanau Peninsula and would recommend staying near Suttons Bay, Leland, or Omena. Northport and Glen Arbor are also lovely, if a bit further away.
