Getting to Suttons Bay

Flying

Flights are available on Delta, American, and United airlines to Traverse City's Cherry Capital Airport, which is around a 45 minute drive from our venue in Suttons Bay.

There are more flight options to airports that are several hours away:

Grand Rapids: 2h45m drive
Detroit: 4h30m drive

Driving

If you live within distance, driving to the area is of course another great option. :)

Addresses for the venue are available on our Wedding Weekend page.

Local Transportation

Uber/Lyft are not reliably available in the Suttons Bay area, so we'd recommend making alternative plans if you're not driving up.

Wedding Shuttle Service

On the day of the wedding, we will have shuttle busses to and from Leelanau Sands Casino, Bayshore Traverse City, and Boardwalk Hotel.

Car Rentals

Rental cars are available at Traverse City airport.

Pre-Booked Taxi Service

Pre-booked taxis are available. Contact these companies for more info: Taxi Service A, Taxi Service B.
