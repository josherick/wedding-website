Getting to Suttons Bay

Flying

Flights are available on Delta, American, and United to Traverse City's Cherry Capital Airport, the closest major airport to our venue. There are also airports a few hours drive with more flight options and lower prices.

We'd recommend flying into one of the following:

  • Traverse City (TVC): 35m drive
  • Grand Rapids (GRR): 2h45m drive
  • Detroit (DTW): 4h30m drive

Driving

If you'd like to drive to the area, our venue's address is available on the homepage.

Local Transportation

Uber/Lyft are NOT reliably available in the Suttons Bay area, so we'd recommend making alternative plans.

Wedding Shuttle Service

On the day of the wedding, we will have shuttle busses to and from Leelanau Sands Casino, Bayshore Traverse City, and Boardwalk Hotel.

Car Rentals

Rental cars are available at Traverse City airport.

Pre-Booked Taxi Service

Pre-booked taxis are available. Contact these companies for more info: Up North Taxi, Traverse City Taxi Cab.

Please contact us at rowleysherickwedding@gmail.com for any wedding related questions.
