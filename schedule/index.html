Ceremony & Reception
3:30pm

The ceremony will begin at 3:30pm. After the ceremony, we'll have a cocktail hour with light bites. Then, we'll move to the barn for a seated dinner, followed by dancing until 10pm!

The dress code is cocktail to formal attire (think suits, dresses, or comparable alternatives). Please avoid wearing shades of white, ivory, or cream, as these colors are reserved for the bride.

Shuttles to the venue will depart from Bayshore Traverse City at 2:15pm, Boardwalk at 2:25pm, and Leelanau Sands Casino at 3:10pm.
