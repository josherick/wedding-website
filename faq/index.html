Frequently Asked Questions

Where are the ceremony and reception?

All wedding events are at Starry Night Barn: 2363 N. Jacobson Rd, Suttons Bay, MI, 49682

The ceremony will be outdoors, weather permitting. The reception will be held in a beautiful barn right next to the ceremony.

What is the schedule for the wedding day (Aug 1, 2026)?

Please arrive around 3pm, and the ceremony will begin promptly at 3:30pm. After the ceremony, we'll have a cocktail hour with light bites. We'll move to the barn for a seated dinner, followed by dancing until 10pm!

What is the dress code?

We request that you wear cocktail to formal attire (think suits, dresses, or comparable alternatives). Please avoid wearing shades of white, ivory, or cream, as these colors are reserved for the bride.

Are plus ones or kids allowed?

Your mailed invitation includes the name of every guest invited, unfortunately additional plus ones cannot be accommodated.

What is there to do/see/eat in the area?

We love the Traverse City/Leelanau Peninsula area and we're so excited to share it with all of you.

If it's your first time in the area, here are some of our favorite things to do:

  • Eat or drink coffee at any of our favorite food and coffee spots
  • Bike along the TART trail from Suttons Bay to Traverse City (rentals)
  • Float on a canoe trip down the Crystal River
  • Climb the the Dunes
  • Wander along one of the many nature walks in Leelanau Peninsula
  • Find a nice beach to post up for reading, swimming, or board games
  • Enjoy a drink at one of the beautiful wineries/cideries/breweries in the area
  • Visit Cherry Republic to see what new cherry-flavored products they've got in store (the area is famous for cherries!)
  • Explore the shops in Fishtown or Traverse City (like Wilson Antiques or Horizon Books)
  • Enjoy the views at Pierce Stocking (drive), Glen Lake (hike), or Pyramid Point (short hike)
  • Drive around to enjoy the country roads, visit farm stands, and go cherry/berry picking

Is there parking available at the venue?

Yes, we'll have plenty of parking available at the venue.

Will there be a shuttle to/from hotels?

We'll have shuttles running at several times to/from Leelanau Sands Casino, Bayshore Traverse City, and Boardwalk Suites (Downtown Traverse City). We may add an additional shuttle stop based on the locations you provide in RSVPs. If you'd like to stay at one of these hotels but our room block is full, let us know and we'll see if we can add more rooms!

Please note that Uber/Lyft are not reliably available in the area, so please see the "Local Transportation" section of our travel page for other options.

Which airport should I use?

TVC is the closest, but GRR and DTW typically have more direct flight options. You can use a service like Google Flights to compare options from your origin.
